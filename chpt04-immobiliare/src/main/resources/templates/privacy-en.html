Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20-01-2025

CasaMia.ai (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data and explains your rights under applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

1.1 Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address

1.2 Behavioral Data

  • User interactions
  • App usage data
  • User preferences

1.3 How Data is Collected

  • User Input: Information provided through forms, sign-ups, and profile updates.
  • Automated Collection: Cookies and analytics tools collect information about your interactions with the app.
  • Third-party Integrations: Data collected via Google or Facebook login.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Core App Functionality: To provide services, including listing rental properties tailored to your preferences.
  • Analytics and Improvements: To monitor and enhance our app’s performance.
  • Marketing and Communication: To send promotional updates or important notifications.

Third-party Sharing

  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics.
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure data storage.
  • OpenAI LLM: For processing user input to deliver tailored property listings.

3. User Rights and Controls

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

3.1 Access, Update, or Delete Data

You can access, update, or delete your data through the Profile section of the app.

3.2 Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent for data usage by deleting your account.

3.3 Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, please use the contact form available on our website or email us at admin@casamia.ai.

4. Data Retention Policy

  • Retention Period: We retain your data until you request deletion.
  • Deletion Process: Users can delete their data through the Profile section. Upon deletion, all personal information will be permanently removed.

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 Use of Cookies

  • Session management
  • Analytics to improve app functionality

5.2 Third-party Tracking

Google Analytics may use cookies to collect information about your interactions with the app.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Encryption:
    • MongoDB Atlas encrypts all cluster storage and snapshot volumes at rest.
    • All communication between the app and MongoDB Atlas is secured using Transport Layer Security (TLS).
  • Secure Access: The app is accessible only via HTTPS.

7. Legal and Regulatory Compliance

7.1 Regions of Operation

CasaMia.ai operates in Europe, with a focus on Romania.

7.2 Applicable Regulations

CasaMia.ai complies with GDPR and other relevant privacy laws based on user location.

7.3 Age Restrictions

CasaMia.ai is not intended for children, but no explicit age restrictions are imposed. The app displays listings of rental properties.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email.
